# Ben Finny — Getting Started (≈15 minutes)

The app is at **https://contractor-north.vercel.app** — works in any browser; on a phone, "Add to Home Screen" to install it like an app.

Your company's data is completely separate from every other company on the system — walled off at the database level. Erik can't see your jobs or customers, and you can't see his.

---

## 1. Create your company (Ben — 5 min)
1. Open the app → **Sign up** with your email + a password.
2. It'll ask you to create a company. Enter:
   - **Company name:** your business name
   - **Your trade:** choose **Tile / flooring** ← this seeds tile job codes (Demo, Substrate prep, Waterproofing/membrane, Backer board, Tile set, Grout/seal, Repair/regrout) instead of generic ones. You can edit/add codes anytime in Settings.

## 2. Set your region — do this first (Ben — 2 min)  ⚠️
**Settings → Company.** The app defaults to Pacific time (it was built in California). Fix three things so your clock, schedule, and invoices are right for New Hampshire:
- **Time zone → `America/New York` (Eastern).** If you skip this, every clock-in and scheduled job reads 3 hours off for your whole crew.
- **State → `NH`.**
- **Default tax rate → `0`.** New Hampshire has **no state sales tax**, so your invoices shouldn't add one. (If you ever do taxable work in another state, set it per-job.)

While you're there, add your **license #**, address, phone, and logo.

## 3. Add your crew (Ben — 5 min)
**Settings → Team → Add employee** for each guy:
- Enter their **name + email** and choose role **Tech** (field employee).
- The app shows a **password once** — text it to that employee.
- They open the app on their phone, log in, "Add to Home Screen," and they're ready.

> You (owner) and any office staff can edit timecards. Techs cannot — that's on purpose (see below).

## 4. Bring in your customers (5 min)
Export your existing clients to a **CSV** (name, address, phone, email). In the app: **Customers → Import CSV** and map the columns.
*(Send Erik the file if you'd rather we run it for you.)*

## How the timeclock works for your guys
Techs are **locked down** so wrong hours can't reach the wrong jobs:
- ⏱️ **Start time** — they can only round the live clock-in **back to the nearest half hour**. No arbitrary times, no backdating.
- 🚫 **No padding** — they **cannot** edit a past entry or add manual hours. You (or office) make corrections.
- 📍 **GPS** is stamped on every clock-in and clock-out, visible to you.
- 🧰 The **job** they clock into is limited to their assigned jobs.

You keep full editing on every timecard.

## Meet Nort
There's a built-in assistant ("Nort," bottom of the screen). Ask it to build an estimate, schedule a job, pull up a customer, or just talk through your day — it figures out you do tile and talks tile. It never sends anything to a customer or moves money without you confirming.
