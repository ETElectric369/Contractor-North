# Chris Wolfe — Getting Started (≈15 minutes)

The app is at **https://contractor-north.vercel.app** — works in any browser; on a phone, "Add to Home Screen" to install it like an app.

Your company's data is completely separate from every other company on the system — walled off at the database level. Nobody else can see your jobs or customers, and you can't see theirs.

---

## 1. Create your company (Chris — 5 min)
1. Open the app → **Sign up** with your email + a password.
2. It'll ask you to create a company. Enter:
   - **Company name:** your business name
   - **Your trade:** choose **General contractor** ← this seeds general job codes (Service call, Install/build, Repair, General labor, Material run, Travel, Cleanup) instead of trade-specific ones. You can edit/add codes anytime in Settings.

## 2. Set your region (Chris — 1 min)
**Settings → Company.** You're in Truckee, so the app's defaults already fit — just confirm and fill in a couple of things:
- **Time zone** — already **Pacific** (`America/Los_Angeles`). Leave it. (Nothing to fix — this is the default.)
- **State → `CA`.**
- **Default tax rate → `0`** — same as ET Electric. As a California contractor on lump-sum jobs you pay tax on materials at the counter, not on the invoice; set a per-job rate only if a specific job needs it.

While you're there, add your **General-B license #**, address, phone, and logo.

## 3. Add your crew (Chris — 5 min)
**Settings → Team → Add employee** for each guy:
- Enter their **name + email** and choose role **Tech** (field employee).
- The app shows a **password once** — text it to that employee.
- They open the app on their phone, log in, "Add to Home Screen," and they're ready.

> You (owner) and any office staff can edit timecards. Techs cannot — that's on purpose (see below).

## 4. Bring in your customers (5 min)
Export your existing clients to a **CSV** (name, address, phone, email). In the app: **Customers → Import CSV** and map the columns.

## How the timeclock works for your guys
Techs are **locked down** so wrong hours can't reach the wrong jobs:
- ⏱️ **Start time** — they can only round the live clock-in **back to the nearest half hour**. No arbitrary times, no backdating.
- 🚫 **No padding** — they **cannot** edit a past entry or add manual hours. You (or office) make corrections.
- 📍 **GPS** is stamped on every clock-in and clock-out, visible to you.
- 🧰 The **job** they clock into is limited to their assigned jobs.

You keep full editing on every timecard.

## Meet Nort
Nort is the built-in assistant — the **sound-wave button at the top-right of the screen**, right next to Search. Tap it to **talk or type**: ask it to build an estimate, schedule a job, pull up a customer, or just talk through your day — it learns your trade from your jobs and meets you there. It never sends anything to a customer or moves money without you confirming.
